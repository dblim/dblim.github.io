Bio

I am a mathematician working in quantitative finance. I received my Ph.D. in math from Stanford in August 2020, advised by Ravi Vakil and Jarod Alper.

My research interests are largely in algebraic geometry, with a focus on moduli theory and derived categories of algebraic stacks. See my research page for more information.

Before coming to Stanford, I was an undergraduate in math at the Australian National University, advised by Jarod Alper and David Ishii Smyth.
